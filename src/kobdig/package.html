The aim of this application is to provide a reference implementation of the KOBDIG deliberating agent model and to demonstrate its mechanics.

KOBDIG stands for Knowledge, Obligations, Beliefs, Desires, Desires, Intentions, and Goals. It is an abstract model of intelligent agent deliberation that has been developed over the years by Célia da Costa Pereira and Andrea G. B. Tettamanzi of the University of Milan.
